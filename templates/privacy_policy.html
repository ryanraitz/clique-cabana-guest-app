Clique Cabana Guest List Manager

Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 23, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Clique Cabana collects, uses, and protects information entered into the Guest List Manager.

Information We Collect

When a team member adds a guest, the app may collect the guest’s first name, last name, phone number, email address, friend count, selected event, selected DJ or team member, SMS confirmation status, and check-in status.

How We Use Information

  • To manage event guest lists and check-ins.
  • To associate guests with the correct event, DJ, or team member.
  • To send optional SMS attendance confirmation messages when phone numbers are provided and messaging is enabled.
  • To sync event and guest information to connected Google Sheets when that integration is configured.

Who Can Access Information

Guest information is intended for authorized Clique Cabana team members only. Access to the dashboard is protected by the team member login/PIN system.

Third-Party Services

The app may use Google Sheets for list syncing and Twilio for SMS confirmation messaging, depending on your configuration. Those services process the information needed to provide their respective functions.

Data Storage

Local app data is stored in the project’s local data file. If Google Sheets syncing is enabled, a copy of event and guest list data may also be stored in the configured Google Sheet.

Data Accuracy and Removal

Team members should enter accurate guest information and remove outdated or incorrect entries when no longer needed for event operations.

Contact

For questions about this Privacy Policy or guest data handling, contact the Clique Cabana event/admin team.
